Rindu

The Night Circus

"The Night Circus" by Erin Morgenstern is a magical, dreamlike novel about a mysterious traveling circus that appears only at night. Two young illusionists are pitted against each other in a contest of skill, but as love grows between them, the stakes of the competition become higher and more perilous. Perfect for readers who love fantasy, romance, and mesmerizing storytelling.
